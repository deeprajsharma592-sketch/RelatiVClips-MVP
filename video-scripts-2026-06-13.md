## 📐 Production notes

### Format cheat sheet

| Format | Length | Cuts | B-roll ratio | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tentpole (1-3) | 90s-3min | Every 2-3 sentences | 30-40% | Launch week, pinned content, ads |
| Daily series (4-10) | 30-60s | Every 1-2 sentences | 20-30% | Recurring cadence, mid-funnel trust |

### B-roll checklist (per shoot)
- [ ] Dashboard stage lights (Transcribe → Score → Detect → Render)
- [ ] Real pipeline output: 3 clips from a real YouTube URL
- [ ] Stats overlay: "3 min · 3 clips · 1 URL"
- [ ] Mock invoice / "$1,500 — 7 days" card
- [ ] Founder's actual workflow screen recordings
- [ ] Before/after: bad auto-clip vs RelatiV output
- [ ] 2am desk setup (for Founder 2am series)

### Visual style
- **Lighting:** natural or single soft key. iPhone native camera, no filters.
- **Framing:** medium close-up, eyes at top third. Vertical 9:16 default.
- **Color:** let the brand carry it. Founder in muted neutral clothing; B-roll in product colors.
- **Text:** Inter or SF Pro, fuchsia accent `#D946EF` for emphasis, white on dark for overlays.
- **Music:** optional, low, fades under voice. Never competing with speech.

### Edit rules
- 2–3 second max shot length on tentpoles, 1-2 seconds on dailies
- Jump cuts on sentence ends, never mid-sentence
- Caption every word (vertical-first audience watches muted)
- Hook must land in first 3 seconds — retake as many times as needed
- B-roll inserts on section breaks
- For #10 (Founder 2am): no cuts, no captions, no music. Raw is the point.

### Voice
- **Speed:** 150 wpm baseline, 170 wpm on future/vision sections
- **Energy:** higher on hook and reveal, lower on problem, medium on how-it-works
- **Tone:** peer-to-peer. "I" for personal, "we" for product. Never "our company" / "users" / "customers."
- **For dailies:** even more casual. Think voice note to a friend, not keynote.

### Cadence (suggested)
- Week 1: shoot tentpoles 1, 2, 3 → launch day
- Week 2: shoot #4 (stress test), #5 (3 things AI can't) → Tue/Thu
- Week 3: ship #6 (devlog) Mon, #7 (hook formula) Wed
- Week 4: ship #8 (200 views) Mon, #9 (MrBeast) Wed, #10 (Founder 2am) Thu
- Repeat weekly with rotated content in the daily series

### Where to film
- **Tentpoles:** clean background, controlled lighting, no distractions. The hook sells; production quality reinforces credibility.
- **Dailies:** anywhere with good audio. Coffee shop, desk, walking outside. Production value is *lower* on purpose — that's the brand.
- **Founder 2am specifically:** at the desk, with the laptop, with the coffee, with the 2am vibe. No staging.
