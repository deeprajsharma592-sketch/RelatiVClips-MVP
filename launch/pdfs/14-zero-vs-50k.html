Script 14: How a $0 brand campaign beat a $50K ad spend
B2B Case Study1:20
FormatStory arc with numbers, founder to camera + B-roll of dashboard/results
AudienceBrand marketers who need proof
Hook styleStory + numbers — concrete proof point

[HOOK — 0:00-0:06] To camera, story-telling posture, slight lean in. A small skincare brand spent fifty-thousand a month on ads. Got eight-hundred-thousand impressions.

[STORY — 0:06-0:35] Slower, narrative. Then they tried something different. They found a creator. Small one. Forty-thousand followers. A skincare creator her audience genuinely trusted. They sponsored one long-form video. A twenty-minute routine. A real conversation. We ran that URL through RelatiV. We got back fifteen clips. Fifteen taste-selected, vertical, captioned, ready to post. The brand posted them on TikTok and Reels. No paid boost. No agency polish. Just the clips. [B-ROLL: dashboard with 15 clips dropping]

[THE RESULT — 0:35-0:55] Energy up, reveal. The result. Two-million impressions. A fourteen-percent engagement rate. Six-thousand comments — most of them tagging friends. All of it from a zero-dollar content budget. The fifty-thousand-dollar ad campaign ran in parallel. It got eight-hundred-thousand impressions. The clips got more than double — for free. [TEXT OVERLAY: "$0 spend · 15 clips · 2M views · 14% ER" vs "$50K spend · 800K views"]

[PAYOFF — 0:55-1:10] To camera, punchline, slight smirk. That's the new math. One URL. Fifteen clips. Beats a fifty-thousand-dollar ad spend. The old playbook is dead.

[CTA — 1:10-1:20] Direct. Your brand. One URL. Link in bio.
