Script 13: Your brand is invisible without clipping
B2B Daily #10:45
FormatFounder to camera, fast pace, single take
AudienceBrand marketers who feel stuck
Hook styleBlunt pain → simple comparison

[HOOK — 0:00-0:05] To camera, blunt. Your brand spends fifty-thousand a month on ads. Nobody remembers it.

[BODY — 0:05-0:30] A creator spends two hours on a video. Posts one clip. Gets two-million views. No ad budget. No agency. Just taste. The math isn't close. Ads interrupt. Clips are content. Interruptions get skipped. Content gets shared.

[CTA — 0:30-0:45] Forward, confident. This is the new playbook. Brands that figure it out will outpace the ones that don't. Clipping isn't a tactic. It's the new ad. Link in bio.
