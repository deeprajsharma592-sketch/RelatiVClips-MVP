Script 12: Clipping is the new advertising
B2B Tentpole3:00
FormatFounder to camera + B-roll + numbers/text overlays
AudienceBrand marketers, growth leads, agencies
Hook styleCategory reframe — "the next ad is the clip"

[HOOK — 0:00-0:08] To camera, deadpan, then lean in. TV ads are dying. Display ads are dying. The next generation of advertising isn't an ad at all. It's a clip.

[OLD WAY IS BROKEN — 0:08-0:35] Slower, exasperated. Here's what the last fifty years of advertising looked like. Brands paid millions for a thirty-second TV spot. They paid billions for display banners. They paid agencies to interrupt you mid-scroll. And every year — the audience got better at ignoring them. Ad blockers. Skip buttons. DVR. The signal-to-noise ratio is collapsing.

[THE PIVOT — 0:35-1:05] Energy up, forward. But something else is happening. The cultural conversation moved to TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. The best brands in the world are no longer buying ad slots — they're becoming content. They're showing up inside the videos their customers already watch. They're sponsoring the creators their audience already trusts. The ad is the clip. The clip is the ad.

[HOW CLIPPING WORKS FOR BRANDS — 1:05-1:50] Demonstrative, structured. Here's the new playbook. Step one — pick a creator your audience already loves. Step two — sponsor a single long-form video. A podcast episode. A YouTube essay. A livestream. Step three — turn that one video into ten, twenty, thirty short-form clips. Step four — distribute those clips across TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and LinkedIn. You don't have an ad. You have a content engine. And every clip is native to the platform. Native gets watched. Native gets shared. Native performs. [B-ROLL: dashboard showing a URL turning into 15+ clips]

[THE MATH — 1:50-2:20] Numbers-forward, confident. Text overlays on each number. Let's talk math. Traditional brand campaign: fifty-thousand dollars. Maybe a million impressions. Two-percent engagement rate. Clipping campaign: five-thousand dollars. Same creator. Same video. Two-million impressions. Twelve-percent engagement rate. Cost per impression drops by ninety percent. Cost per engagement drops by ninety-five percent. That's not an incremental improvement. That's a category change. [TEXT OVERLAY: "$50K → 1M views · 2% ER" → "$5K → 2M views · 12% ER"]

[THE RELATIV ROLE — 2:20-2:45] To camera, brand-positioning. And this is exactly what RelatiV is built for. We're the infrastructure layer between long-form creators and the short-form world. Brands don't have to hire an agency. Brands don't have to brief a creator. Brands don't have to wait seven days for a team of editors. Paste a URL. Get the clips. Ship the campaign. The new ad isn't an ad. It's a clip. And we're building the rails.

[CTA — 2:45-3:00] Direct, hold eye contact. If you're a brand spending more on ads every year and getting less from them — let's talk. Link in bio. First ten brands get a free pilot.
