# Funding Programs (8 targets)

> **8 specific programs. Each gets: name, format, check size, fit score, how to apply.**

---

## 1. Y Combinator (YC)
- **Format:** 3-month batch in SF (or remote), $500k standard deal
- **Check size:** $500k for 7% post-money SAFE
- **Application cycle:** Twice a year (W26 batch applications usually open Aug-Sep)
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High if we have early users. YC loves "infrastructure" + "creator economy" angles.
- **Apply:** ycombinator.com/apply
- **What they want to see:**
  - Live product (we have it)
  - 1% week-over-week growth (we don't have this yet)
  - Founder velocity (we have it — 3 days from idea to live)
  - A clear "why this, why now" narrative

## 2. Pioneer (by a16z)
- **Format:** 8-week virtual program, $50k grant, community
- **Check size:** $50k grant (no equity)
- **Application:** Rolling
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High — they specifically fund pre-traction technical founders
- **Apply:** pioneer.app/apply
- **Bonus:** Access to a16z network, weekly founder calls

## 3. OnDeck Founders
- **Format:** Community + fellowship, $250k+ from partner funds
- **Check size:** Variable (often $200-500k via partner funds)
- **Application:** Rolling
- **Fit for RelatiV:** Medium-high — community-focused, great for solo founders
- **Apply:** beondeck.com/founders

## 4. Afore Capital
- **Format:** Pre-seed fund, $250-500k checks
- **Check size:** $250-500k
- **Application:** Rolling, intro preferred
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High — Afore specifically does pre-seed, SaaS thesis
- **Apply:** afore.vc (need warm intro)
- **Sweet spot:** Show 50+ paying users

## 5. Antler
- **Format:** 6-month residency, $250-500k investment
- **Check size:** $250-500k
- **Application:** Rolling
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High — Antler has a strong India presence (Antler India, ex-NEA)
- **Apply:** antler.co/apply
- **Bonus:** Office + community + India-specific programming

## 6. Sequoia Surge (India/SEA)
- **Format:** 16-week program, $1-2M seed investment
- **Check size:** $1-2M for ~10% equity
- **Application:** Annual (cohort usually starts Q1)
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High if we have India traction; Surge specifically targets India/SEA
- **Apply:** sequoiacap.com/surge
- **Sweet spot:** Show India user base

## 7. 100X.VC
- **Format:** Indian pre-seed fund, $200-500k checks
- **Check size:** $200-500k
- **Application:** Rolling
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High — 100X is the most active Indian pre-seed
- **Apply:** 100x.vc

## 8. Microsoft for Startups / Google for Startups
- **Format:** Cloud credits (~$25-100k), sometimes cash
- **Check size:** $25-100k in credits, no equity (sometimes)
- **Application:** Rolling
- **Fit for RelatiV:** High for v1 launch — covers our $11/mo Hetzner for YEARS
- **Apply:**
  - startups.microsoft.com
  - startups.google.com
- **Bonus:** Azure / GCP credits can be used for our backend

## My recommended order (based on fit + speed)

1. **Pioneer** — easy application, no equity, fast decision. Apply in 24h.
2. **Microsoft / Google for Startups** — free credits, easy approval. Apply in 24h.
3. **Antler** — Indian presence, good for solo founders. Apply in 1 week (need to prep deck).
4. **Afore Capital** — need a warm intro. Work the network for 2-3 weeks.
5. **Y Combinator** — strongest signal, hardest to get into. Wait for W26 deadline (Aug-Sep).
6. **OnDeck, 100X.VC, Sequoia Surge** — backup options.

**Estimated total time to first $50k raised:** 2-4 weeks if we hustle.
**Estimated time to first $500k raised:** 8-12 weeks with traction + intros.
