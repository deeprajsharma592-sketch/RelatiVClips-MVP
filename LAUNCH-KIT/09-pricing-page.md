## FAQ

**How does the 7-day free trial work?**
Pro is free for 7 days. No credit card to start. We'll email you 3 days before the trial ends. Cancel anytime in one click.

**What counts as "video processing time"?**
Just the time our pipeline is actively running on your video. Upload time and download time don't count. If your video is 30 minutes, the plan's minute budget is reduced by 30.

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes, anytime. Upgrades are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Do you offer annual pricing?**
Not yet. We're keeping it simple for v1. Email founders@relativ.app if you want a yearly plan and we'll add you to the early-access list.

**What if I go over my minute budget?**
We'll email you at 80% and 100%. At 100%, new processing jobs are queued until next month. Old jobs are never lost.

**Can I bring my own AI models?**
Pro and Teams plans support a self-hosted Claude endpoint (Anthropic-compatible API). See the [docs] for setup.

**Is there a free trial for Teams?**
Yes, 14 days. Email sales@relativ.app with your team size.

**Do you offer refunds?**
Pro-rated refund within the first 30 days, no questions asked.
