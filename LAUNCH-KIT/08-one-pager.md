## What

RelatiV is the **infrastructure of authenticity** for the AI-slop era.

A short-form video engine that turns one YouTube URL into 3-5 ready-to-publish clips in 60 seconds. We use Claude for taste-based hook scoring (not just energy peaks), YOLO for face tracking, and a self-hostable open-source engine that runs on a single €10/month VPS.

## Why now

- **AI slop is winning the feed.** TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts — 60%+ AI-generated. Creators are burning out.
- **The signal is reversing.** TikTok now deprioritizes AI-flagged content. FTC passed AI disclosure rules in 2025.
- **MrBeast, Casey Neistat, others** are explicitly building "the counter-move" — real creators, real infrastructure, real moments.

## Why us

- **The infrastructure is the moat.** Most competitors wrap OpenAI APIs in a UI. We built bgutil PO-token provisioner, Deno JS runtime, multi-region routing — the actual hard parts of fetching YouTube at scale.
- **Taste, not just speed.** Claude scores every hook moment for emotional peak, narrative completeness, shareability. Quality is the moat, not volume.
- **Self-hostable.** 100% open-source engine. The founder's principle: own the infra, don't depend on third parties.
